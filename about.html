Who I Am

I am a SharePoint and M365-focused software developer with a strong interest in building secure, reliable collaboration solutions for enterprise environments. I specialize in translating complex technical requirements into solutions that are easy for end users and administrators to understand and support.

My work emphasizes availability, security, and maintainability—especially in regulated or mission-driven organizations where reliability matters.

  • Communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical users

Technical Focus Areas

  • Power Automate workflows and connector-based integrations

Enterprise Mindset

I approach development with an enterprise mindset—considering long-term maintenance, security posture, and user impact. I understand that successful solutions are not just functional, but also supportable, auditable, and aligned with organizational standards.

I am comfortable working closely with administrators, vendors, and customers to troubleshoot issues, manage expectations, and continuously improve collaboration tools.

Why SharePoint & M365

SharePoint and the Power Platform provide powerful, secure building blocks that allow organizations to move fast without sacrificing governance. I enjoy leveraging these tools to solve real business problems while staying aligned with modern DevSecOps principles.
